FAVORITE TEAM

JAPAN

I like the Japan men's volleyball team because their players are shorter than most other volleyball players around the world. Even though they are not as tall, they still play very well with great teamwork and strong spirit. Japan is also the only Asian country that represents Asia in the Olympic Games. Watching them play really inspires me, and I dream of playing with them one day in the future.
